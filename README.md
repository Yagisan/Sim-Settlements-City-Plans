# Sim Settlements City Plans by Yagisan

This is a collection of City Plans for [Sim Settlements](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/21872/) for Fallout 4.

It's goal is to provide a variety of themed City Plans, that are realistic and mostly lore friendly.

## Requirements
### Required DLC
* Automatron
* Wasteland Workshop
* Far Harbor
* Contraptions Workshop
* Vault-Tec Workshop
* Nuka-World

## Categories
Plans are divided into 2 categories.
[PCSM] are PC only plans, that require mods for significant structural work. These are Nexus only mods.
[XB1] are Xbox One compatible city plans. They rely on vanilla and DLC structures, and may take advantage of Creation Club materials. They are available for both PC and Xbox One.

It also has a robot recycling and junk bot construction facilities, and a first class kitchen. It will eventually need to import water. Includes a small player home. Supports 15 settlers. Recommended to assign Supervisor White as mayor, and the robots to the 5 farms.
